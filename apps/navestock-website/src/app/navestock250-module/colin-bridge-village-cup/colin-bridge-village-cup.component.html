Navestock Cricket Club

Colin Bridge Villagers Cup

Wednesday 20th June 2018

The Colin Bridge Villagers Cup took centre stage on the Wednesday and with five teams competing in an exciting 6-a-side tournament for the trophy.

The 6 teams competing for the trophy were as follows:

  • Matching Green (holders)
  • Navestock
  • Abridge
  • Fyfield
  • Theydon Bois

The final was contested between Theydon Bois and Matching Green. With Matching Green winning the match with 4 balls to spare.

Congratulations to Matching Green on retaining the trophy.
